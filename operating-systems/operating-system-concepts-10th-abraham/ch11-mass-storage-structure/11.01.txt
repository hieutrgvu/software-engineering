11.1 Is disk scheduling, other than FCFS scheduling, useful in a single-user
environment? Explain your answer.

********************************************************************************
Resolved by: Hieu Vu
********************************************************************************

Yes. Applications in single-user enviroment can request a lot of disk accesses.
Dis scheduling other than FCFS such as SSTF, SCAN, C-SCAN, etc. can optimize
the movement of read/write head.
