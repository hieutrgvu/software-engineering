9.12 Which of the following sequences are heaps?

a. 42 35 37 20 14 18 7 10
b. 42 35 18 20 14 30 10
c. 20 20 20 20 20 20

+ "a" and "c" are heaps.
+ "b" is not a heap because 18 is less than 30.
